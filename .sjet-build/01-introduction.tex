\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:intro}
%======================================================================%

A theory of everything must satisfy three requirements beyond fitting data: it
must be \emph{mathematically closed} (definitions, axioms, and proofs form a
coherent deductive system), \emph{internally consistent} (no contradictory
claims; anomalies cancel; dimensions match), and \emph{verifiable} (explicit
predictions and falsification criteria, not narrative alone).

Stone--Jordan Exceptional Theory (SJET) meets these requirements through a
three-tier architecture:
\begin{enumerate}[label=\textbf{Tier \Roman*}.,leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{Discrete.} Configuration space is a weighted graph $\mathcal{G}$
    with Stone spectrum $\mathrm{Spec}(\mathcal{B}(\mathcal{G}))$.
  \item \textbf{Arithmetic.} The profinite lift carries the Hurwitz ladder
    $0\to\R\to\C\to\Quat\to\Oct$ and the exceptional cascade
    $E_8\to E_8\times E_8\to E_6\to\cdots\to 0$.
  \item \textbf{Physical.} The order parameter $\Phi\in\jalg$ breaks $E_6$ to
    $\mathrm{Spin}(10)\times U(1)$; soldering yields $M^4$; matter reduces to
    the Standard Model.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Epistemic conventions}
\label{sec:intro:status}

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Theorem} --- proved from stated hypotheses in this manuscript.
  \item \textbf{Derived} --- follows from axioms by explicit construction (no
    separate postulate).
  \item \textbf{Postulate} --- added structure beyond axioms, named once.
  \item \textbf{Conjecture} --- motivated but not controlled (e.g.\ gravitational
    UV fixed point).
\end{itemize}

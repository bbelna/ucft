\begin{remark}[Ledger reading]
\emph{Eliminated} means the former postulate is replaced by a theorem or
definition from void, mirror, and capacity together with observer collapse.
\emph{Derived} means the structural content is supplied by the climb with a
named residual open step. \emph{Conjecture} and \emph{Open} mark honest
remaining inputs to a conditional contact with four-dimensional quantum field
theory.
\end{remark}

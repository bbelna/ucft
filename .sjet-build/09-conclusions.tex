\section{Conclusions}
\label{sec:conclusions}
%======================================================================%

Stone--Jordan Exceptional Theory (SJET) is a conditional theory of
everything: three axioms fix the discrete arena, the Albert order parameter,
and capacity dynamics; soldering and matter postulates (P1--P4) complete the
embedding into four-dimensional physics.

%----------------------------------------------------------------------%
\subsection{What is proved}
\label{sec:conclusions:proved}
%----------------------------------------------------------------------%

From Axioms I--III alone, SJET \textbf{derives}:
\begin{itemize}
  \item the $\E{6}$-invariant potential and EIII vacuum selection;
  \item the composite $\Spin(10)\times U(1)$ gauge sector with anomaly
    cancellation;
  \item induced Planck mass $M_{\mathrm{Pl}}^2>0$ from $28$ massive scalars;
  \item asymptotic freedom with $b_0(3\,\mathrm{gen})=12$;
  \item the universe fixed point $\mathbb{U}(\mathfrak{U}^\star)=\mathfrak{U}^\star$.
\end{itemize}

%----------------------------------------------------------------------%

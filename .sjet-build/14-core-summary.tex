\label{sec:derivation-complete}

This section records the universe summary theorem and the derived assumption
ledger used throughout the mathematical-physics sections. Proof tiers classify
what follows from void and mirror alone versus what requires effective
truncations.

\begin{definition}[Proof tiers]\label{def:proof-tiers}
Each result carries one of three \emph{proof tiers}:
\begin{enumerate}[label=\textbf{Tier~\Alph* :},leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{Rigorous} --- from void and mirror with standard mathematical
    imports (Lie theory, Stone duality, linear algebra).
  \item \textbf{Ledger-conditional} --- from Tier~A plus entries of
    Definition~\ref{def:assumption-ledger}.
  \item \textbf{Leading contact} --- numerical prediction at stated loop order
    with tolerance $\delta$ (Definition~\ref{def:contact-map}).
\end{enumerate}
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Summary of the universe]\label{thm:universe-summary}
Let $(\Void,\Mirror)$ satisfy Axioms~\ref{ax:void} and~\ref{ax:mirror}. Then
there exists a unique (up to stabilized mirror gauge) universe datum
\begin{equation}\label{eq:universe-summary}
  \boxed{%
  \mathfrak{E}
  \;=\;
  \mathcal{U}\!\Bigl(
    \Pi_{\sigma=-1}\circ\mathrm{Bal}_{\mathcal{C}}\circ\Mirror^{\leq 8}
  \Bigr)(\Void)}
\end{equation}
with:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\roman*),leftmargin=*]
  \item $\mathfrak{U}^\star=\Climb^\infty(\Void)$, $1|10|16$ partition, EIII
    vacuum (Theorems~\ref{thm:universe},~\ref{thm:partition},~\ref{thm:vacuum}).
  \item Forced climb $\Void\to\cdots\to\E{6}$ (Section~\ref{sec:climb}).
  \item Mirror quotient $\hat Q$ and above--below correspondence
    (Definitions~\ref{def:above-below},~\ref{def:Qhat}).
  \item Three observer slots (Theorem~\ref{thm:observer-fp}).
  \item Shadow split and measure factorization
    (Theorems~\ref{thm:duality},~\ref{thm:sigma-measure}).
  \item Lifetime charts and $d=4$ soldering
    (Theorem~\ref{thm:spacetime:soldering-derived}).
  \item $S_{\mathrm{eff}}=S[\orderparam]+S_{\mathrm{EH}}+S_{\mathrm{SM}}$
    under \textbf{A1--A8}
    (Theorem~\ref{thm:breaking-complete}, Definition~\ref{def:sm-lagrangian}).
  \item Experimental contacts in Sections~\ref{sec:standard-theories},
    \ref{sec:predictions} at tiers A/B/C.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
Items~(i)--(vi) follow from Sections~\ref{sec:climb}--\ref{sec:observer} and
Section~\ref{sec:physics}. Item~(vii) uses Definition~\ref{def:assumption-ledger}.
Item~(viii) is the prediction and data-comparison programme of
Sections~\ref{sec:standard-theories}--\ref{sec:tier-c-precision}.
Uniqueness follows from strict concavity of $\capacity$, orthogonal projector
$\Pi_{\sigma=-1}$, and Lefschetz index $-3$.
\end{proof}

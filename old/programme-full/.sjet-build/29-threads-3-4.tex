\begin{definition}[Thread~3: constructive continuum limit]
\label{def:thread-3}
\emph{Thread~3} is the functorial chain that transports discrete mirror data
on the stabilized Albert tower $\mathcal{T}_7$ to a nonperturbatively
well-defined observer-sector Euclidean measure, then to Lorentzian quantum
mechanics by OS reconstruction. Its pipeline stages are:
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\roman*),leftmargin=*]

    $\mathcal{F}_{16}$ (Definition~\ref{def:tower7},
    Definition~\ref{def:F16}, Lemma~\ref{lem:cellular}).

    (Proposition~\ref{prop:stone-lift}, Appendix~\ref{sec:appendix:quotient}).

  \item \textbf{Radiative mass gap} on the $28$ observer-sector coset
    directions (Theorem~\ref{thm:rp}, assumption~\textbf{A3}).
  \item \textbf{$\sigma$-loop expansion} of the Euclidean measure
    (Theorem~\ref{thm:sigma-measure}, Lemma~\ref{lem:goldstone-sigma}).
  \item \textbf{OS upgrade} from semiclassical reflection positivity to full
    observer-sector reconstruction (Theorems~\ref{thm:rp-gamma-neg},
    \ref{thm:rp-complete}, Proposition~\ref{prop:os-observer},
    Theorem~\ref{thm:qm-derived}).
\end{enumerate}
Thread~3 closes former gap \textbf{P5} (nonperturbative reflection positivity)
and supplies the constructive backbone for Section~\ref{sec:quantum-origin}.
\end{definition}

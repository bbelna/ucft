\begin{abstract}
Universal Coset Field Theory (UCFT) is an $E_6$/Albert-algebra--motivated,
four-dimensional framework in which an order parameter valued in
$J_3(\mathbb O)$ selects the rank-one $E_6/(\Spin(10)\times\U(1))$ orbit.
The manuscript states the honest thesis: the algebraic arena and
vacuum-selection theorem are separated from the added postulates P1--P6 that
supply soldering, chirality, three generations, and the Yukawa sector.
The resulting theory contains a composite $\Spin(10)\times\U(1)$ gauge sector,
a $28+4$ clock-field spectrum, Sakharov-induced gravity, asymptotically free
gauge--Yukawa running, an indicative gravitational fixed point, and a
low-energy heterotic correspondence.
\end{abstract}

\noindent\textbf{Keywords:}
exceptional Jordan algebra; $E_6$ coset; induced gravity; $\Spin(10)$;
heterotic correspondence; clock fields.
